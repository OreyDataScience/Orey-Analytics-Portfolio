# Retail SME POS Data – Insight Summary

## Dataset Overview
The cleaned dataset contains retail Point-of-Sale (POS) transactions that have been processed and standardized for analysis.  
All inconsistencies, missing values, and formatting issues have been resolved, making the data reliable and analysis-ready.

---

## Key Observations

- **Total Revenue**  
  Total revenue was successfully calculated from all transactions, providing a clear view of overall business performance.

- **Top Performing City**  
  The city generating the highest revenue was identified, highlighting the strongest geographic market for the business.

- **Top Selling Category**  
  The best-performing product category was determined based on sales volume/revenue, indicating customer demand trends.

- **Most Common Payment Method**  
  The most frequently used payment method was identified, offering insight into customer payment preferences.

- **Sales Activity Over Time**  
  Sales patterns across the entire period were analyzed, revealing trends such as peak sales days and fluctuations in activity.

---

## Conclusion
The dataset is now clean, consistent, and structured for deeper analysis.  
It can be confidently used for business intelligence, dashboard creation, and data-driven decision-making.
